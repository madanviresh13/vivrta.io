What you receive

Every Vivrta.IO report includes

01

Executive Brief

A half-page leadership summary in plain English: what the code does, the overall risk rating, and the three most important actions for your leadership team. Written for a Finance Director, not a developer.

02

Detailed technical findings

Every risk rated RED, AMBER, or GREEN with evidence from the code, business impact, verification steps, and a required fix. No guesswork, no generic advice.

Remediation scope estimate

Total developer days to fix every RED and AMBER finding, broken down by category with contingency built in. Give your project manager something concrete to plan with.
